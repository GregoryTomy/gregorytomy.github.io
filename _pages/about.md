I am a final-year Master's student at the University of Colorado Boulder, specializing in Applied Mathematics with a focus on Statistics and Machine Learning. Currently engaged in an independent study on Causal Inference and its applications in Data Science, I am keenly interested in leveraging my academic foundation to solve real-world problems. **As I approach graduation in May 2024, I am actively exploring full-time opportunities in Data Science and Machine Learning Engineering, where I can contribute to and grow with forward-thinking organizations**.

My professional journey has afforded me the opportunity to work across a variety of sectors, including technology manufacturing at GlobalFoundries, research at Sandia National Labs, healthcare analytics at CVS Health, and financial analysis at Brean Strategic Advisors. These experiences have not only enriched my technical toolkit—spanning XGBoost, PyTorch, BigQuery, and Python—but have also honed my ability to apply machine learning models and data analysis techniques to drive impactful business and research outcomes.

My background is characterized by a blend of strong analytical skills, technical proficiency, and a strategic approach to problem-solving. I am passionate about the potential of machine learning and data science to transform data into insights, drive innovation, and create value.
